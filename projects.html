SAMREU

SAMREU was an NSF-funded mathematics research program held at Sam Houston State University during the summer of 2014. As a research assistant I worked on extending the results of several published papers in the field of Monoid Theory. In particular I studied lengths of irreducible factorizations of Arithmetic Congruence Monoids. The program culminated in a written report, currently being edited for submission.

4-command CPU

The final project for CSCE 312 (Computer Organization) was to design an assembly language and a CPU, as well as writing a program adding two 2 by 2 matrices in the language. The digital logic for the CPU was implemented in Logisim. Download
